After a good start at the box-office, Sara Ali Khan and Vicky Kaushal\u2018s romantic comedy Zara Hatke Zara Bachke has witnessed a slight drop on its first Monday. The film, which opened to mixed reviews from critics, earned around Rs 4.41 crore on Monday, taking its total earnings to Rs 26.73 crore.<\/p>\n

Sharing the latest box office figures, film trade analyst Taran Adarsh wrote on Twitter, \u201c#ZaraHatkeZaraBachke keeps its investors smiling on the crucial Day 4 [Mon]\u2026 The strong grip on a working day \u2013 after the weekend \u2013 indicates the wholehearted acceptance for the film\u2026 Fri 5.49 cr, Sat 7.20 cr, Sun 9.90 cr, Mon 4.14 cr. Total: \u20b9 26.73 cr. #India biz.\u201d<\/p>\n

He added, \u201c\ud83d\udc4d\ud83c\udffb Mass pockets \/ single screens are super-strong\u2026 National chains are rock-steady too [weekday rates]. \ud83d\udc4d\ud83c\udffb Decline on Mon [vis-\u00e0-vis Fri] is mere 24.59%, which is excellent.\u201d<\/p>\n

Zara Hatke Zara Bachke, which was said to have profited from its unique ticket-selling strategy of one plus one free, valid until Sunday, performed decently at the box-office on Monday without any sales aid.<\/p>\n

Directed by Laxman Utekar, Zara Hatke Zara Bachke, set in a small town in India, revolves around a married couple trying to get a fake divorce. The film marked Vicky Kaushal and Sara Ali Khan\u2019s first film together and fans can\u2019t have enough of their cute chemistry. It has been able to hold strong despite competition from The Kerala Story and Spider-Man: Across The Spider-Verse.<\/p>\n

In her review of the film, Indian Express\u2019 Shubhra Gupta stated that the film has the potential to relate to the Indian middle class because of its central theme. She wrote, \u201cZara Hatke Zara Bachke\u2019s central conflict is one that makes it instantly relatable to millions of middle-class Indians. When it comes right down to it, young Indore-based couple Kapil (Vicky Kaushal) and Soumya (Sara Ali Khan) have some tough calls to take: will they be able to go through with it?\u201d<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
